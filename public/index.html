Live DDoS Map

This application showcases ScyllaDB's power in handling and analyzing high-intensity cyber activities. It's specifically designed to simulate tracking of DDoS (Distributed Denial of Service) attacks, demonstrating ScyllaDB's ability to efficiently manage thousands of operations every second. The core feature involves recording a large volume of simulated DDoS events into ScyllaDB, which can then be analyzed and visualized through real-time data retrieval -- also from ScyllaDB.

Take a look at the various ScyllaDB performance metrics, noting the high performance and predictable low latency achievable with just one node. Enjoy linear performance at scale as you go beyond this simple demo and run ScyllaDB with your own application.
